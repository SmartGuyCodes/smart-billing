# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is Smart Billing?

Smart Billing is a solution designed to streamline and automate your billing and invoicing processes, making it easier to manage payments, track transactions, and generate reports.

## How do I install Smart Billing?

Please refer to the [Installation Guide](./installation.md) for step-by-step instructions on setting up Smart Billing in your environment.

## What are the system requirements?

- PHP 8.3 or higher
- Composer
- MySQL or PostgreSQL database
- Node.js (for frontend assets, if applicable)

## How do I create and send invoices?

Navigate to the Invoices section in the dashboard, click "Create Invoice," fill in the required details, and use the "Send" option to email the invoice directly to your client.

## Can I customize invoice templates?

Yes, Smart Billing supports customizable invoice templates. Check the documentation under [Customizing Templates](./customization.md) for more details.

## How do I handle recurring billing?

Recurring billing can be set up by enabling the "Recurring" option when creating an invoice or subscription. You can manage frequency and duration in the invoice settings.

## Is my data secure?

Smart Billing uses industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Ensure your server and dependencies are kept up to date for maximum security.

## How do I get support?

For support, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/SmartGuyCodes/smart-billing) or contact our support team at smartguycodes@gmail.com.

## Can I integrate Smart Billing with other systems?

Yes, Smart Billing provides API endpoints and webhooks for integration with other applications. See the [API Documentation](./api.md) for more information.

## How do I update Smart Billing?

Run `composer update` in your project directory to update to the latest version. Always back up your data before performing updates.
